I know you think that that’s part of every person’s childhood, but mine was different due to some of the reasons. Some of them are

But you know a child is always tend to do that thing more that we stop them from doing. So me and my two best friends used to stay outside until 10. It was thrill that drives us and i miss that thrill that we used to have.
